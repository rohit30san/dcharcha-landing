1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Majji Digital Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (“Dcharcha”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you access our mobile application, website, or any related digital service (collectively referred to as the “Platform”).

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all users who access or use the Dcharcha Platform, including citizens, civic partners, and collaborators. By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, and phone number (optional)
  • Profile information such as display name or photo
  • Device and technical information (IP address, operating system, app version)

3.2 User Submissions

  • Reports, images, or videos uploaded through the Platform
  • Location metadata for accurate civic issue mapping

3.3 Analytics Data

  • Usage patterns, session duration, and app interactions
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and enhance Dcharcha’s features and user experience
  • To process and analyze civic issue reports
  • To ensure platform security, performance, and accuracy
  • To communicate with users regarding updates or civic engagement reports
  • To conduct analytics and research in an aggregated or anonymized manner

5. AI-Generated Content

Dcharcha utilizes AI technologies to visualize and analyze civic issues. These AI-generated outputs are intended for informational and representational purposes only and do not constitute verified government data or endorsements.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade user data. Your information may be shared only under the following circumstances:

  • With government or civic authorities for verified issue resolution
  • With trusted third-party service providers (such as Google Maps, Firebase, or OpenAI) for technical operations, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or governmental request, in compliance with applicable regulations

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time
  • Request information on how your data is used or shared

Requests to exercise these rights can be sent to info@dcharcha.com.

8. Data Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to ensure complete data security, no system can guarantee absolute protection.

9. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by applicable law. After that period, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. The revised version will be posted on our website with the updated date at the top. Continued use of our Platform after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
